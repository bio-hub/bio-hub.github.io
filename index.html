Services and analyses

#4

#5

Curating literature

Based on syntax analysis, we cluster published articles into:

1. Diagnosis
2. Treatment & Prevention
3. Epidemiology
4. Transmission
5. Clinical, signs & symptoms.

Why we do

Our efforts is to mine and translate scientific COVID-19 information to help our government.
